**Q:** How good is Geti'ikku?
**A:** The weapon is good on single-target, however it loses value in most multitarget situations due to secondary stats being better.

**Q:** Are there any alternate talents for Mythic+?
**A:** Bladestorm is a very strong choice in Mythic+, and will generally lead to better results. 
Reckless Abandon is also very strong for more infrequent but much stronger burst. 
Anger Management can be used to pair Recklessness with Bladestorm every time. 
Inner Rage is a good choice on specifically Explosive weeks in order to ensure you can kill the Explosive orbs.

**Q:** Are there alternate talents for Battle of Daz'alor?
**A:** Bladestorm is a good choice for Mythic Opulence for more damage to the adds in Phase 2.
Reckless Abandon is a potential choice for Mythic Rastakhan and Mythic Mekkatorque.
Anger Management is a potential choice for Mythic Jaina as this allows you to use Recklessness on the wall as well as using it twice on Jaina.

**Q:** Are there stat breakpoints?
**A:** There are no stat breakpoints that you should be specifically aiming for. Just follow your top gear sims instead of worrying about specific stats.

**Q:** What potion do I use?
**A:** Bursting Blood for pure single-target, Strength Pot for everything else.
