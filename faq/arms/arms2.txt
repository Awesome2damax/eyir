**Q:** Is Mortal Strike used in the Execute phase?
**A:** At this point no, the DPR of Execute and MS with 2 OP stacks is basically even. However in practice it's better to keep using Execute as it doesn't slow the rotation as much.

**Q:** Do you wait for 40 rage to cast Execute?
**A:** No, you want to use execute at any rage, the DPR scales linearly.

**Q:** What is the general stat priority?
**A:** Haste > Crit > Strength > Versatility > Mastery
__THIS CAN VARY FROM CHARACTER TO CHARACTER PLEASE SIM FOR MORE ACCURATE WEIGHTS__

**Q:** Are there any breakpoints we need to aim for?
**A:** No, there are tons of breakpoints that are not impactful outside of a sim. Keep getting more Haste and Crit.

**Q:** Is Avatar or In for the Kill better for M+?
**A:** In for the Kill is generally better, however Avatar can be used with Blood of the Enemy if you coordinate with your group for large pulls.

**Q:** Is Fervor of Battle viable in M+?
**A:** No, Massacre is still much better for M+

**Q:** Rend/FoB sims higher, should I use it over Massacre?
**A:** Yes, massacre has generally fallen out of favour since 8.2 Essences. 

**Q:** Is Second Wind Viable?
**A:** Yes, in some cases in raid "Environmental" damage doesn't break Second Winds healing. Ex: Mythrax and Bursting/Greivous in M+.

**Q:** What Potion does Arms use?
**A:** Unbridled Fury for single target, Greater Strength for 2-3, Proximity for 4+

**Q:** What are the best Trinkets for Arms?
**A:** Since Titanforging makes a lot of gear relevant, you can check out the Bloodmallet list here. - <https://bloodmallet.com/index.html#warrior_arms>

**Q:** How is Razor Coral used for Arms?
**A** The trinkets second use is best saved for the Execute + Lucid Dreams window.

**Q:** How is Font of Azshara used for Arms?
**A:** You would precast it before pull, then use it on cd with Lucid. However it is always best to save a use of it for the Lucid Dreams + Execute window.
