**__FAQ__**

**Q:** What are the best traits?
**A:** 
Single-target: Test of Might x3 > Heart of Darkness > Meticulous Scheming > 1 Crushing Assault/Striking the Anvil 
AoE with Warbreaker: Lord of War > Gathering Storm 
AoE with Cleave: Test of Might > Gathering Storm 

**Q:** With only 1 Test of Might trait, do I still delay Bladestorm till the Test of Might Window?
**A:** Yes, even with 1 trait only it's still worth to delay Bladestorm. On Burst AoE it's fine to ignore the ToM trait.

**Q:** Does Crushing Assault work with Fervor of Battle?
**A:** Yes, the trait reduces the rage cost of Whirlwind by 20 and increases the Slam cast damage.

**Q:** When do I use the Crushing Assault proc?
**A:** Before Execute use it like you would use Slam/Whirlwind. In Execute you want to use Slam even with Fervor of Battle selected, it as soon as it procs.

**Q:** What are the best Essences for Arms?
**A:** Single Target: Lucid Dreams major, Focusing Iris, Conflict and Strife, Breath of the Dying, Unbound Force.
AoE with Warbreaker: Focusing Iris Major, Lucid Dreams, Breath of the Dying, Conflict and Strife minors.

**Q:** Is it ok to sit on Skullsplitter?
**A:** Yes, the power of Skullsplitter comes from the near on demand rage. Sitting on it's cooldown during lucid or high rage is fine.

**Q:** Are there any alternative talents in Ny'alotha?
**A:** The only talents that ever change is the 75 row. Collateral Damage is preffered for 2 target cleave and more boss damage. Cleave is used for AoE damage and for periods of 3+ target cleave. Warbreaker is more burst aoe oriented.

**Q:** Is Mortal Strike used in the Execute phase?
**A:** No, Mortal Strike is not used in Execute.
